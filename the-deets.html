DETAILS

Transportation

On the day of the wedding, a complimentary shuttle will be provided for guests staying at The Inn in Celebration. The bus will depart from the hotel at 2:45 PM and arrive at Bella Collina around 3:45 PM for the festivities. At the end of the night, the same bus will pick guests up at 9:45 PM for the return trip to The Inn.

Accommodations

We’ve arranged a room block at The Inn at Celebration for guests who’d like a convenient and comfortable place to stay. You’re also welcome to book accommodations anywhere in the Orlando area—there are plenty of great options nearby to suit different preferences.

Wedding Itinerary

The weekend will begin with a relaxed Welcome dinner on Thursday, followed by the wedding day celebration at Corpus Christi and Bella Collina. For anyone who wants to keep the magic going, there will also be an optional day at Disney after the wedding.
